What to expect from classes?

Our teaching methods combine of classroom instruction, lectures, workshops, study groups, excersises, major project work and guidance. We put utmost pressure on keeping our practices relevant and in accordance to the theory. Through all of the aforementioned you as a student is supposed to develop your independence as well as collaborative and innovative skills.
